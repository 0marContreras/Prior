About Prior

Get started

Prior is a platform where the students can upluoad their integrative projects, get feedback from other students and teachers and also be evaluated in real time by the major judges.

At the same time, Prior has a space where students can publish new ideas for future projects or for people who like share their own crazy ideas.

What can I do in prior?


In prior you and your team can upload the integrative project into a space where another members of the scholar community can comment and rate your project. Prior was made to make faster the evaluation process of all the integrative projects in the univerity giving to the evaluators a tool where they can evaluate the project durning the presentation.

Prior provides a space where any student can share new ideas with all the scholar community no matter the quarter where the student is.

Who can use prior?


Prior can be used for anyone in the university. Don't hold yourself back!
